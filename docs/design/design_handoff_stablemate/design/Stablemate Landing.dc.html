How it works

Live in three steps, no agent to install

If your job can make an HTTP request, it can be monitored. That's the whole integration.

1Create a monitor

Name it after your job and set the interval — say every 1h with a 5-minute grace.

# you get a unique URL
stablemate.dev/ping/8f3a…
2Ping it from your job

Add a one-line request at the end of the work. Curl, Net::HTTP, or our tiny helper.

$ curl -fsS \
  stablemate.dev/ping/8f3a…
3Relax — we watch

No ping past the grace window? The monitor flips to down and you get an email instantly.

Built for Solid Queue

Your recurring.yml is the spec

Stablemate speaks Solid Queue natively. Each recurring task maps to a monitor, and its Fugit cron schedule becomes the expected interval — so a missed scheduler run is a missed heartbeat.

Auto-import every task from your schedule with one command
Schedule changes sync — intervals stay in lockstep with your cron
Pause a queue in Solid Queue, pause the monitor — no false alarms

production:
daily_digest:
class: DigestMailerJob
